Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a problem with the app?

You can report any technical issues by contacting our support team through the contact form above, or by emailing support@kusahflbillness.co.tz. Please include details about your device, operating system version, and a description of the problem.

How can artists submit their music to your platform?

Artists can submit their music by emailing artists@kusahflbillness.co.tz with their press kit, music samples, and relevant information. Alternatively, they can work with our verified distributors listed in the "Artists Distributors" section.

What are the benefits of premium membership?

Our premium membership offers ad-free listening, unlimited skips, offline downloads, higher audio quality, and exclusive content. You can upgrade through the app or website at any time.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you'll need to cancel through those platforms. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for verified students. To qualify, you'll need to verify your student status through our partner verification service. The discount applies as long as you remain a student.
