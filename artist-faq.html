Artist Frequently Asked Questions

Uploading & Distribution

How do I upload my music to Kusah FL Billness?

To upload your music, you need to create an artist account and go to the "Upload" section in your dashboard. From there, you can upload your tracks, add metadata (title, artist name, genre, etc.), and submit for review. Once approved, your music will be distributed across our platform.

What file formats do you accept for upload?

We accept high-quality audio files in WAV (44.1kHz/16-bit minimum) or FLAC format for the best sound quality. We also accept MP3 files (320kbps preferred). The file size limit is 500MB per track.

How long does it take for my music to go live?

After submission, our team reviews all content to ensure it meets our quality standards and guidelines. This process typically takes 3-5 business days. Once approved, your music will be live within 24-48 hours.

Can I schedule my release for a specific date?

Yes! Our premium artist accounts allow you to schedule releases up to 6 months in advance. This feature is perfect for planning your marketing campaigns and building anticipation for your new music.

Payments & Royalties

How do I get paid for my streams?

We pay royalties monthly based on your streams from the previous month. Payments are processed by the 15th of each month and are sent via bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), or PayPal, depending on your preference.

What's the payment rate per stream?

Our payment rates vary based on the listener's subscription type (free or premium) and country. On average, artists earn between TZS 5 to TZS 20 per stream. Premium subscribers generate higher royalty rates than free users.

Is there a minimum payout threshold?

Yes, the minimum payout amount is TZS 50,000 for bank transfers and TZS 10,000 for mobile money payments. If your earnings don't reach this threshold in a given month, they will roll over to the next month.

How can I track my earnings?

Your artist dashboard provides real-time analytics showing your streams, earnings, and listener demographics. You can access detailed reports broken down by day, track, and region to help you understand your audience better.

Promotion & Growth

How can I get my music featured on Kusah FL Billness?

Our editorial team regularly reviews new music for playlist placement. To increase your chances, ensure your profile is complete, your music is high quality, and you're actively promoting your releases. You can also submit your tracks for playlist consideration through your artist dashboard.

What are Verified Artist badges and how do I get one?

Verified badges help distinguish official artist profiles. To qualify, you need to have at least 3 tracks uploaded, a complete profile with bio and photos, and demonstrate you're the legitimate rights holder. Apply through the "Get Verified" section in your account settings.

Can I run ads to promote my music on your platform?

Yes! Our self-service advertising platform allows you to create targeted campaigns to promote your music to relevant listeners. You can set your budget, target by location, age, listening habits, and more. Visit the "Promote" section in your dashboard to get started.

What tools do you offer to help me grow my audience?

We provide several tools including: pre-save campaigns for upcoming releases, smart links to track clicks, embeddable players for your website, detailed analytics, and the ability to message your followers directly through our app. Premium artist accounts get access to additional promotional features.

Account & Technical

How do I update my artist profile information?

You can edit your profile at any time by going to "Profile Settings" in your artist dashboard. Changes to your artist name may require verification to prevent impersonation. Profile picture and bio updates typically go live within 24 hours.

What should I do if I find unauthorized content under my name?

Immediately report the content through the "Report" button on the track/page or contact our Copyright team at copyright@kusahflbillness.co.tz with proof of ownership. We take copyright infringement seriously and will investigate promptly.

Can I transfer ownership of my artist account?

Yes, but this requires verification from both parties to prevent unauthorized transfers. Contact our Artist Support team with both parties' identification and a signed transfer agreement to initiate the process.

How do I delete my artist account?

Account deletion is permanent and will remove all your content from our platform. Before proceeding, download any data you wish to keep. To delete, go to Account Settings > Advanced > Delete Account. Note that royalties from past streams will still be payable according to our normal schedule.

Still have questions?

Our artist support team is here to help you with any additional questions or concerns you may have about using our platform.
