Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a link to create a new password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

Why can't I play certain songs?

Some songs may be unavailable due to licensing restrictions in your region or if the artist has temporarily removed the content. Try checking back later or explore similar music from our extensive catalog.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your Account Settings > Subscription. Follow the prompts to cancel. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How many devices can I use with one account?

You can use your Kusah FL Billness account on up to 5 different devices. However, you can only stream music on one device at a time with a standard subscription. Consider our Family Plan for multiple simultaneous streams.

How do I download music for offline listening?

With a Premium subscription, you can download songs by clicking the download icon (↓) next to any track, album, or playlist. Downloaded content is available in the "Downloads" section of your library for offline playback.
